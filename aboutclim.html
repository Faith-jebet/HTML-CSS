Causes of climate change and its effects

Causes of climate change

The following are the causes of climate change

  • Climate change is primarily caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation

  • Industrial processes that release greenhouse gases into the atmosphere

Effects of climate change

Climate change can have several effects on farmers; this may include:

  • Increased frequency of extreme weather events like droughts and floods, shifts in growing seasons, and the spread of pests and diseases.
  • These factors can impact crop yields, soil quality, water availability, and overall agricultural productivity.
  • It's important for farmers to adapt to these changes and implement sustainable practices to mitigate the effects of
